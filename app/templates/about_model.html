About the Model

PitchIQ is designed as a transparent sports analytics system. The goal is not to produce a black-box prediction, but to show which factors contribute to a matchup projection.

Planned Factors

  • Recent pitcher form across the last several starts
  • Season-level pitching performance
  • Pitcher history in the current stadium
  • Batter-vs-pitcher history against the projected lineup
  • Opponent lineup performance against pitcher handedness
  • Weather impact: temperature, humidity, wind, pressure, and precipitation
  • Expected innings and quality start probability

Planned Outputs

  • Pitcher advantage
  • Team win probability
  • Expected innings
  • Strikeout projection
  • Quality start probability
  • Model confidence

PitchIQ is a sports analytics portfolio project. It is not gambling advice.
